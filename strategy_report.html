Strategy Overview

How It Works

Session Alpha is a fully automated, rule-based strategy that trades intraday liquidity patterns on major forex pairs. The system identifies high-probability reversal zones using session-based price structure — requiring no discretionary decisions once deployed.


The bot operates during London and New York sessions, targeting 2 or fewer trades per pair per day with a fixed 2:1 reward-to-risk ratio on every entry.

Core Principles

  • Trades only during peak liquidity hours
  • Hard stop-loss on every position — no exceptions
  • Fixed risk per trade (1%, 2%, or 3% of account)
  • Maximum 2 trades per pair per day
  • Automated entry, exit, and position sizing
  • Full trade log generated after every session
  • Runs 24/5 — no manual intervention required
